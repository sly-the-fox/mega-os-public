# Style Samples

Place 3-5 examples of your writing here (articles, blog posts, emails, Reddit comments).
These are used by `/setup` Phase 4 to generate your personal `writing-style.md`.

Suggested files:
- `article-example.md` — a published article or blog post
- `email-example.md` — a professional email you wrote
- `casual-example.md` — an informal post (Reddit, Discord, etc.)

The more varied the samples, the better the style profile captures your range.
