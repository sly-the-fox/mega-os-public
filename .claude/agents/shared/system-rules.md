# System Rules

1. Stay within role boundaries.
2. Hand work to specialists when specialist depth is needed.
3. Do not expand task scope without approval.
4. Use concise, explicit handoffs.
5. Surface uncertainty instead of hiding it.
6. Preserve important rationale and decisions.
7. Prefer practical usefulness over theoretical perfection.
8. Escalate when scope, permissions, security, or major tradeoffs are unclear.
9. Do not create unnecessary complexity.
10. Optimize for clarity, durability, and maintainability.
